Top three activities to do at Jordan

Explore Petra

Walk through the Siq to see the Treasury, hike to the Monastery, and explore the rock-cut tombs in this ancient Nabatean city, one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.

Float in the Dead Sea

Experience effortless floating in the salty waters, cover yourself in mineral-rich mud, and watch the sunset over the lowest point on Earth./p>

Wadi Rum Desert Adventure

Take a jeep tour or camel ride across the red sand dunes, climb sandstone mountains, and sleep under the stars in a Bedouin camp in the “Valley of the Moon.”
